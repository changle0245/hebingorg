隐私政策

最后更新：2026年4月

🔒 核心承诺：你上传的所有文件均在浏览器本地处理，不会上传到我们的服务器，我们无法访问你的文件内容。

1. 我们收集什么信息

hebing.org 是一个浏览器端文件处理工具，我们收集的信息极为有限：

  • 访问日志：通过 Google Analytics 收集匿名访问数据，包括页面访问量、地理位置（国家/城市级别）、使用的浏览器和设备类型。这些数据无法识别个人身份。
  • 订阅邮件：如果你主动填写邮件地址订阅更新，我们会保存该邮件地址，仅用于发送新工具上线通知。
  • 文件数据：你上传的文件完全在你的浏览器内处理，不经过我们的服务器，我们无法获取任何文件内容。

2. 我们如何使用信息

  • Google Analytics 数据用于了解网站使用情况，改善工具体验
  • 订阅邮件仅用于发送新工具上线通知，不会用于商业推广或出售给第三方
  • 我们不做任何形式的用户画像或行为追踪

3. Cookie 和追踪

我们使用 Google Analytics 的 Cookie 进行匿名统计分析。这些 Cookie 不包含个人身份信息。你可以通过浏览器设置拒绝 Cookie，不影响工具的正常使用。

4. 广告（Google AdSense）

本网站展示 Google AdSense 提供的广告。Google 作为第三方供应商使用 Cookie（包括 DART Cookie）根据你在本网站及其他网站的访问情况投放广告。这些 Cookie 可能包含你的 IP 地址、浏览器类型、访问时间、访问页面等信息，但不含个人身份信息。

你可以选择退出个性化广告：

  • 访问 Google 广告设置 管理个性化广告偏好
  • 访问 aboutads.info 退出多家第三方广告商的 Cookie 追踪
  • 欧盟/英国/瑞士地区用户：首次访问时会弹出 Cookie 同意对话框（由 Google 认证的 CMP 提供），你可以拒绝个性化广告

更多细节请查阅 Google 广告隐私政策。

5. 数据安全

由于我们不存储你的文件，文件安全风险极低。订阅邮件仅保存在你本机浏览器的 localStorage 中，不上传到我们的服务器。

6. 第三方服务

  • Google Analytics — 匿名访问统计
  • Google AdSense — 广告展示
  • Cloudflare — 网站托管和 CDN
  • unpkg / jsDelivr / cdnjs — JavaScript 库（pdf-lib、SheetJS 等）CDN 加载

7. 你的权利

你可以随时要求删除你的订阅邮件，发送邮件至 hello@hebing.org 即可。

8. 联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请联系：hello@hebing.org

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

🔒 Core commitment: All files you upload are processed locally in your browser and are never sent to our servers. We cannot access your file content.

1. Information We Collect

hebing.org is a browser-based file processing tool. The information we collect is minimal:

  • Access logs: Anonymous usage data via Google Analytics, including page views, geographic location (country/city level), browser and device type. This data cannot identify individuals.
  • Email subscriptions: If you voluntarily submit your email address for updates, we store it solely for sending new tool launch notifications.
  • File data: Files you upload are processed entirely within your browser and never pass through our servers. We cannot access any file content.

2. How We Use Information

  • Google Analytics data is used to understand site usage and improve the tool experience
  • Subscription emails are used only to notify you of new tool launches, never for marketing or sold to third parties
  • We do not create user profiles or conduct behavioral tracking

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use Google Analytics cookies for anonymous statistical analysis. These cookies contain no personally identifiable information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting tool functionality.

4. Advertising (Google AdSense)

This website displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (including the DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. These cookies may contain your IP address, browser type, visit time and pages viewed, but no personally identifiable information.

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Manage preferences at Google Ad Settings
  • Opt out of third-party vendor cookies at aboutads.info
  • EU/UK/Swiss visitors: A cookie consent dialog (provided by a Google-certified CMP) appears on first visit, allowing you to decline personalized ads

For more details, see the Google Ads Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

Since we do not store your files, file security risk is minimal. Subscription emails are saved only in your browser's localStorage and never sent to our servers.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — Anonymous visit statistics
  • Google AdSense — Advertisement display
  • Cloudflare — Website hosting and CDN
  • unpkg / jsDelivr / cdnjs — JavaScript library CDN (pdf-lib, SheetJS, etc.)

7. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your subscription email at any time by emailing hello@hebing.org.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact: hello@hebing.org
